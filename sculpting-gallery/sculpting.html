Sculpting Using PalTiya Clay

Clay and classes

I teach sculpting classes throughout New Zealand using PalTiya clay (www.paltiya.com). As you can see we’ve had great fun recently with an Artweek community build that was held in Devonport – over 300 people attended during the week we were doing this!

As well as teaching classes and facilitating large group sculpture builds, I am also a reseller of the clay in the Auckland area. Please contact me on 021944556 or janet.mazenier@gmail.com if you wish to buy the clay.

A half day class will result in participants being confident about producing a small sculpture using the PalTiya sculpting processes. Participants can take their sculpture home (together with their 4kg pail of clay).

A full day class allows participants to produce two or more sculptures, or one large-ish sculpture which can be taken home.

Class prices are agreed with participants depending on whether a half or full day class is required. All materials are included in the class price.

Prices for this range from $80-$160 per person. Participants will take home their sculpture/s plus clay. Note that any venue hire charges are in addition to this.
