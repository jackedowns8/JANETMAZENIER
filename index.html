About

Janet Mazenier explores the human condition in its response to space.. She investigates how we respond to spatial elements such as lighting, volume and how we participate in the areas we inhabit.

Janet resides in Devonport, Auckland and holds a Bachelors Degree in Fine Arts.

She has participated in many individual and group exhibitions and in 2016 has been a finalist in the Cliftons Art Prize as well as the Parkin Drawing Prize.
