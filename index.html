About

My artistic practice investigates how the human condition is constructed, affected and altered by its experiencing of physical space.

As such, I explore understandings of personal/psychological and collective/social experience of space and spatiality. Incorporated into this is a questioning of how memory influences our perception of space within the realms of design, architecture and aspects of mass media.

Using paint, photography and sculpture, I am evolving my practice to a multi-disciplinary, inter-disciplinary focus.

My paintings and photography are represented by the Flagstaff Gallery in Devonport, New Zealand.
