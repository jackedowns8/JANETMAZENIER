About

Janet Mazenier is a full-time visual artist who exhibits widely and has work in collections in New Zealand, Australia and the United Kingdom. Prior to taking up painting full-time, she was a business person who enjoyed leading large programmes of work within the public sector, information technology or telecommunications industries. Janet continues to take a programme approach to her painting, identifying and investigating the relationship between humans and the architecture of their spaces.
